DESIGN

I design with a focus on user experience, ensuring that those visiting your site are engaged and can navigate the site effortlessly. The design is so much more than how a site looks - it is also how it functions for the user.

DEVELOPMENT

In developing any site, I use the latest technology available in a way to ensure that the content looks great regardless of what device it is being viewed from. The manner in which the modern web is engaged in requires every site to be responsive, looking great on a home computer, tablet, or phone.

CONTENT

I create content consistent with any client's branding needs, ensuring a great first impression. Web content communicates 24/7, and great content equals greater traffic and engagement from any target audience.

THE WEB COMMUNICATES

A website provides a great way to communicate with any potential audience. Your site will work to get out whatever you need to communicate 24/7, and provides engagement in a way that other marketing simply falls short of.

A STREAMLINED PROCESS

First, we can work together consider the purpose of the project, and from there, the scope. Moving forward I can develop mockups and a prototype for you to evaluate. Pending approval, a site can then be developed and deployed for anyone to engage with.

A SITE BASED ON YOUR NEEDS



The design of any components of a website should be based around its purpose. Need to convey a strong message and convert a customer? A focused landing page can be developed to meet such needs. Or do you need to be able to continue to provide your own content or blog posts on the site? A CMS (Content Management System) is great, and I love to work with Wordpress. A brochure style website consisting of multiple pages? No problem, I can design a main page template and develop from there.
